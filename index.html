Song Library

Store your full song library with multiple parts, slides, and CCLI license info. Search and add to playlists in seconds.

🖥

Dual Screen Control

Separate Front of House and Back of House windows. The operator sees what's coming next while the congregation only sees what's sent.

📽

Media & Sermons

Images, videos, PDFs, and PowerPoint slides — all in one media library. Add sermon slide decks to your playlist alongside songs.

☁️

Cloud Synced

Your playlists and songs live in the cloud. Log in on any computer and your entire service is ready to go.

⌨️

Keyboard Driven

Navigate slides, toggle backgrounds, send blackouts, and control logos — all without touching the mouse during service.

⚡

Instant Setup

No installation. Open a browser, log in, connect your Firebase project once, and you're live. Works on any OS.

Getting started

Up and running
in three steps.

01

Create your account

Sign up with an email and password. Then connect your own Firebase project — your data stays in your project, not ours.

02

Build your library

Add songs with lyrics split into parts and slides. Upload media items for sermon content. Everything is searchable and reusable week to week.

03

Go live on Sunday

Build your service playlist, open the FOH and BOH screens, click Go Live, and use your keyboard to control what the congregation sees in real time.
